INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS

  1. A fresh copy of the Content Profiles - Cover Photo, Banner & Site Branding Plugin will be installed on your server! Please take a backup of your site's code and database before you continue. If you do not know how to take Backup of your site, then you may refer to the Backup and Restore Plugin
  2. Please configure your license from the "Files" section of your Client Area on SocialEngineAddOns.
  3. If you have not installed the SocialEngineAddOns Core Plugin on your site, then please download the latest version of this free plugin from your Client Area on SocialEngineAddOns and install it.
  4. Log into your SocialEngine 4 site and access the Admin area.
  5. Access the "Manage" menu and click on "Packages & Plugins".
  6. Click on the "Install New Packages" link, and then on the "Add Packages" link.
  7. Select the TAR file that accompanies this file.
  8. Follow the step-by-step wizard to complete the installation of the plugin files.
  9. After successful installation of this plugin, go to the Content Profiles - Cover Photo, Banner & Site Branding Plugin "Global Settings" page and activate your plugin there.
    10. That's it!
